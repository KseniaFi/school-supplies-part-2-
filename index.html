The Fruit Drawing Contest

A Story of Tom and Anna

This is Tom and Anna. They are classmates.

Today is a special day at school. Hurrah! It is the "Fun Fruit Drawing Contest!"

The teacher says, "Your mission is to draw the ripest red apple! The winner's drawing will be on the school album cover. Ready? Go!"

Tom opens his pencil case. He wants to draw a big, round apple. But oh, no! He can't find his favourite pencil!

Anna turns to her friend. "What's wrong, Tom?" she asks. "I forgot my lucky pencil at home", says Tom. "Can I borrow your pencil, please?" "Of course," says Anna. "Here you are."

Anna gives Tom her sharp spare pencil.

Tom is happy. He starts drawing.

He draws a circle for his apple. It is almost perfect!

Oh, dear! He makes a smudge on the paper! Tom looks at the clock. Only thirty seconds left! He needs help. "Can I borrow your rubber, please?" he asks. "Sure," says Anna. "Here you are."

Tom uses Anna's cloud-shaped rubber. He rubs out the smudge. "Thank you," says Tom. "You're welcome," says Anna.

Tom finishes his drawing just in time. Hurrah! Anna is happy to help her friend.

Tom's drawing is definitely the best in the class! Hurrah!
